ABOUT ME

Having more than 7 years in The Fashion Industry, Navya Ramesh has worked with over a hundred brands in the fashion, retail, ecommerce, beauty, makeup, and grooming and lifestyle space. She is an independent content creator, model, presenter, blogger and writer with a sizeable social media following. She has also been a guest writer publishing various articles on the latest fashion trends, personal style and fashion weeks. Her expertise in the field drives her to share her knowledge and experience in hopes of elevating one’s personal and professional life.
